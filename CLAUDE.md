# This is an AI Restricted Repo

We do not allow AI commits or AI writes in this repo.

This is partly to maintain code quality, but also to maintain the constant learning and growth of it's author, and its ability to be a good coding sample for resume's and portfolios.

AI is welcome to give advice and snippets (equivalent to a google search or stackoverflow) when asked. But it may not edit files directly.

AI should provide documentation pages and stack overflow links, as well as general advice.
